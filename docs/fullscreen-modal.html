Fullscreen Modal

Full-screen modal dialogs for immersive experiences like onboarding, media viewers, and complex forms.

Basic Fullscreen Modal

Modal Title

This is a fullscreen modal. It takes over the entire screen for an immersive experience.

Great for:

  • Onboarding flows
  • Media viewers
  • Complex forms
  • Mobile-first experiences
<div x-data="uxFullscreenModal()"> <button @click="open()">Open Modal</button> <div class="ux-fullscreen-modal" :class="{ 'ux-fullscreen-modal--open': isOpen }"> <header class="ux-fullscreen-modal__header"> <div class="ux-fullscreen-modal__header-start"> <button class="ux-fullscreen-modal__close" @click="close()">X</button> </div> <div class="ux-fullscreen-modal__header-center"> <h2 class="ux-fullscreen-modal__title">Title</h2> </div> <div class="ux-fullscreen-modal__header-end"> <button>Save</button> </div> </header> <div class="ux-fullscreen-modal__content">...</div> <footer class="ux-fullscreen-modal__footer">...</footer> </div> </div>

Multi-Step Modal

<div x-data="uxFullscreenModal({ totalSteps: 3 })"> <!-- Step dots in header --> <div class="ux-fullscreen-modal__steps"> <template x-for="step in totalSteps"> <div class="ux-fullscreen-modal__step-dot" :class="{ 'ux-fullscreen-modal__step-dot--active': isStepActive(step - 1), 'ux-fullscreen-modal__step-dot--completed': isStepCompleted(step - 1) }"></div> </template> </div> <!-- Navigation --> <button @click="prevStep()">Back</button> <button @click="nextStep()">Next</button> </div>
